<objective>
Fix the CSV schema mismatch between the pipeline and manuscript, and add Bayesian power
validation (HDI-excludes-zero check) to posterior contrasts.

Purpose: The manuscript silently falls back to N/A for all inline statistics because
(1) pipeline CSV filenames include model suffixes that the manuscript does not expect,
(2) per-parameter contrasts are split into separate files instead of one group_contrasts.csv,
and (3) HDI column names use hdi_3/hdi_97 while the manuscript queries hdi_3%/hdi_97%.
Additionally, the ROADMAP success criterion 1 requires detection of the simulated interaction
but the pipeline provides no mechanism to assess this. Adding hdi_excludes_zero provides
the Bayesian equivalent of "statistically significant."

Output: Updated group.py, 06_group_analysis.py, paper.qmd, and tests.
</objective>
